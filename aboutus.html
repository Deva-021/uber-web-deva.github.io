Moving people and the world forward

Welcome to Uber

Uber is revolutionizing the way people move. Since 2009, we've transformed transportation by connecting riders to drivers through our app. What started as a simple idea to tap a button and get a ride has become a global movement that's changing how cities move.

Today, we operate in over 10,000 cities worldwide, providing safe, reliable, and affordable transportation options. Whether you're commuting to work, heading to the airport, or exploring a new city, Uber is there to make your journey seamless.

Our Story

Our Mission

We believe transportation should be as reliable as running water, everywhere for everyone. Our mission is to reimagine the way the world moves by connecting riders to drivers at the touch of a button.

By making transportation more accessible, we're creating opportunity, bringing people together, and making cities more livable. We're not just a ride-sharing company - we're a technology platform that's changing the fabric of urban mobility.
